In another universe,

I never replied to that message.

You seemed distant.

Protective.

Almost rude.

I could have walked away.

But in this universe, I stayed.

You came to Bangalore.

I made you dosa on the first day.

You were skeptical.

But you stayed.

One month together.

Shivasamudra.

Isha Foundation.

Nandi Hills.

We weren’t pretending anymore.

Then came Wayanad.

That trip was proof.

We were building something.

You went home.

But we didn’t drift.

We chose each other.

Convincing everyone wasn’t easy.

There were doubts.

But we didn’t step back.

I flew to Delhi.

Pre-wedding shoot.

Seeing you again was breathtaking.

Engagement in Aligarh.

You in gold.

I was mesmerized.

5th March 2024. Haldi.

Dance. Laughter. Chaos.

6th March 2024.

You walked toward me in red.

I felt like the happiest man alive.

Scientist in ISRO.

Alignment.

Sakleshpur. Udupi.

1000 km in rain.

Completely wet. Completely alive.

Ahmedabad.

Dwarka. Somnath. Nashik.

Daman. Mumbai.

After Mumbai came the news.

You are pregnant.

Our universe will expand.

There are infinite universes

where this never happened.

Every universe is wrong.

Except this one.
